An Akan name generator is a tool used to automatically give Akan names based on specific cultural rules, mainly the day of the week a person was born. Akan names come from the Akan people of Ghana, and each name carries cultural meaning and identity.

Akan names are based on the day of the week in which one was born in in the Ghanian culture.Their meaning often showing the diffrence between males and females .
